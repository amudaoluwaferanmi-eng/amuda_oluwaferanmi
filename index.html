How long does a website take? +
Depending on the scope and complexity, most websites take a few days to a week to complete. I'll give you a clear timeline once we discuss your project.
Do you redesign existing brands? +
Yes, absolutely. I help brands improve their visual identity, refine their logo, update their colour palette, and elevate their overall online presence.
How do I contact you to start a project? +
The easiest way is through WhatsApp using the "Let's Talk" button above. You can also send an email to amudaoluwaferanmi@gmail.com and I'll respond within 24 hours.
What are your payment terms? +
I typically require a 50% deposit before starting and the remaining balance on delivery. Full payment may be required for smaller or rush projects.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes. The number of revisions depends on the service package. Most packages include at least 2 revisions. The premium combo package includes unlimited revisions.
Can I see examples of past work? +
Of course! Visit my portfolio at afcreativedesign to see designs, websites, and ebooks I've created for past clients.
